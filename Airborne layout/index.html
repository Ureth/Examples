Welcome to Airborne Trampoline Woodbridge

Why choose us?
Airborne Trampoline is a recreational & competitive trampoline facility open to the public and the gymnastics community alike. All of Airborne’s coaches are certified by N.C.C.P. and Gymnastics Ontario. Airborne Trampoline is a proud member of Gymnastics Ontario & Gymnastics Canada.
